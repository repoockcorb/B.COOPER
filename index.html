Let me introduce myself.

I'm a Mechatronics Engineer who is passionate for designing new technologies while learning and building on a set of skills i have been developing for numerous years through university, work and personal projects. I have worked on number of projects with a variety of companies from small one off prototyping to batch manufacturing of parts.
